PYTHAGORAS

Known as: “Pythagoras of Samos”

Birthdate/Place: Born on 570 BC on the island of Samos, Greece

Died:495 BC (at the age of 75) in Metapontum Village, Greece

Nationality:Greek

Famous for:Pythagorean Theorem

Family Background:

Spouse/Ex-: Theano

Father: Mnesarchus

Mother: Pythais

Children: Arignote, Damo, Myia, Telauges


Pythagoras, sometimes known as Pythagoras of Samos, was an ancient Greek mathematician and philosopher. Today, many people are familiar with Pythagoras because of the famous Pythagorean Theorem, which is a mathematical formula for finding the length of the hypotenuse of a right triangle.
